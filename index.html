ABOUT US

To Improve access to Healthcare and promote Public Health.

We are Nigeriaâ€™s foremost non-governmental organisation focused on using digital technology in addressing the toxic mix of problems that has crumbled the Nigerian healthcare system including inaccessibility of quality healthcare, poor health infrastructure, and lack of proper public health promotion.

Our innovative free telemedicine consultation and medication supply directly benefit thousands of Nigerians with more operational projects spread across 932 remote villages and slums in the 36 states of Nigeria. Floy foundation believes that members of the civil society needs to be fully involved in the impact we are making. Our model of society driven change spurs us to continue sensitising and engaging the civil society, making it an active partner in all we do to drive change that is needed and improve health and lives of the under-served, despite their social and financial challenges.

We believe our approach would help reach out to more people and reduce the elevated mortality rate caused by limited access to healthcare.
